Story

How it all started

The story begins with Jimmy. Jimmy is a 10-year-old who lives on a large farm with his grandparents. Jimmy and his grandparents are part of whats left of the worlds population after the pandemic wiped out a third of the Earths population, including Jimmys parents. Jimmy spends most of his time with his dog Daisy.

One day while exploring the woods on the farm, Jimmy finds a strange object buried in the ground. He tries to pull it out of the ground to no avail. But here comes Daisy, she manages to dig around the object and what Jimmy originally thought to be a weird object turns out to be a part of a spaceship. Upon further exploration Jimmy with the help of Daisy makes his way into the spaceship where he is greeted by a creature he has never seen before.

Daisy seems to like the creature so Jimmy calls him Bunnie. Over time, Jimmy and Bunnie become friends. Jimmy finds out that there are other creatures like Bunnie trapped in various locations across the Earth and he decides to rescue them all. Jimmy with the help of Bunnie and Daisy manage to free a lot of other Bunnies.

As the species grew, the humans become uncomfortable. The humans call the species BULLYBUNNIES. Unfortunately, the humans make it clear that the BullyBunnies are not welcome to stay on Earth. The humans are scared and they want the BullyBunnies gone ASAP. Will the bunnies be able to survive being attacked by the humans or will they all be exterminated? Find out in the BullyBunnies game.
